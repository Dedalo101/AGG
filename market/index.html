Market Analysis & Trends

Strong Fundamentals

The market demonstrates remarkable resilience with international buyers representing one in three purchases in Málaga province, the highest proportion ever recorded. Transaction volumes remain robust at 2,339 in Q1 2025 for the Golden Triangle, approaching the record highs of 2022.

Growth Projections

Industry experts forecast continued but measured growth for 2025-2026:

  • Core zones (Sierra Blanca, Golden Mile): 4-7% annual appreciation
  • Transition areas (East Marbella, Nueva Andalucía): 6-9% annual growth
  • Reclassified PGOM zones: 10%+ potential appreciation by 2026
