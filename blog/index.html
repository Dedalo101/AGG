üìä Investment Outlook: 2024-2026

Our market analysis indicates continued strong performance for the Costa del Sol real estate market, driven by several key factors:

Positive Market Drivers

  • Infrastructure Development: New high-speed rail connections to Madrid and improved airports
  • Limited Supply: Strict planning regulations limiting new construction in prime areas
  • Quality of Life: Excellent climate, healthcare, and international schools
  • EU Citizenship: Spain's Golden Visa program attracting high-net-worth individuals

We predict continued price appreciation of 8-12% annually for prime properties, with luxury segments potentially outperforming. Rental yields are expected to remain strong at 5-8% for well-positioned properties.

üí° Expert Investment Tips

1. Location is Everything

Focus on properties within walking distance of beaches, with sea views, or in exclusive golf course communities. These locations consistently outperform the broader market.

2. New vs. Resale

New developments offer modern amenities and warranties but command premium prices. Resale properties in prime locations can offer better value and immediate rental potential.

3. Rental Investment Strategy

Properties near golf courses, beaches, and amenities typically achieve higher rental yields. Consider proximity to airports for easy access by short-term renters.

4. Legal Due Diligence

Always work with qualified legal professionals familiar with Spanish property law. Ensure all permits and licenses are in order before purchase.

5. Currency Considerations

Monitor exchange rates if purchasing from outside the Eurozone. Consider hedging strategies for large transactions.
